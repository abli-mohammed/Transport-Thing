Welcome to our website !

Our website is for helping people to send their goods, documents and other stuff, from one place to another place for a fee or free at any time.

You can add an order by specifying the place of departure and arrival, date and type of delivery.

When adding an order, we wait for recipients to accept the request, then choose between them.

After selecting the owner of the delivery, the communication takes place by phone or email.

helping people to send their goods

Most of us would like to send goods, documents or anything else, but we cannot move on our own and through our website, we make it easier for them to choose someone else to deliver their items for money or for free.
